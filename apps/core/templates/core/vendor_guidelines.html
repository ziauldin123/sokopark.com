Vendor Guidelines

Account Requirements

  1. Full registration certificate (RDB).
  2. Momo merchant code.
  3. Bank account details.
  4. High resolution company logo.

Vendor Policy

  1. The vendor must provide detailed & accurate information.
  2. The vendor must respect the intellectual property of others. If you feel someone has violated your intellectual property rights, you can report it to Sokopark.
  3. The vendor should always act fairly and not misuse Sokopark's features or services.
  4. It is the responsibility of the vendor to update the products information (stock, delivery times).
  5. Product images must be in high definition and the main image must have a white background.
  6. The vendor should not attempt to increase the price of a product after an order is confirmed.
  7. The vendor should not operate more than one selling account.
  8. The vendor should not attempt to circumvent Sokopark's sales process.
  9. The vendor should not send unsolicited or inappropriate communications.
  10. The vendor should not share customer information such as addresses or phone numbers with any third-party.
